Roleplaying Etiquette

  • Avoid Powergaming: Don't force your character's actions or abilities onto others without their consent. Roleplay should be collaborative, not competitive.
  • No Metagaming: Do not use out-of-character (OOC) information to your advantage in-character (IC). What your character knows should not be influenced by what you, the player, know.
  • Be Patient: Roleplay can be slow-paced at times. Be patient and allow others to respond to your actions and messages.
  • Personality & Character: Maintain a clear personality for your character throughout roleplay interactions, unless there's a valid in-character reason for a change. Keep your character's appearance, behavior, and actions with the server's theme and setting and your own character backstory.
  • OOC and IC Separation: Clearly distinguish between out-of-character (OOC) and in-character (IC) conversations and actions. Use brackets or labels if necessary.
